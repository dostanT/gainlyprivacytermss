2. What Gainly Does

Gainly is a fitness progress tracking app that lets you:

  • Log workout sessions, sets, reps, and RPE-based effort ratings
  • Build a workout rotation and follow AI-generated training plans
  • Track body measurements, nutrition, hydration, and daily wellbeing
  • Photograph your physique and receive AI-powered analysis scored across Symmetry, Proportions, Conditioning, and Development
  • View progress over time through charts, trends, streaks, and personal records
  • Optionally sync workout and body data to Apple Health (Pro feature)

4. Subscription and Billing

4.1 Free Tier

Free users receive 1 lifetime AI physique analysis and access to core workout logging, nutrition tracking, and progress charts. This one-time analysis is provided so you can experience the feature before upgrading. Usage limits are subject to change with advance notice.

4.2 Pro Subscription

Pro unlocks 5 AI physique analyses per week (resetting every Monday), AI-generated training plans, the full workout template library, AI nutrition scanning, advanced scheduling for workout rotation, cycle-aware training recommendations, Apple Health sync, extended analytics, and CSV export.

  • Trial: 7 days free. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, the subscription renews automatically at the applicable price.
  • Billing: Charged through your Apple ID on a recurring monthly or annual basis, depending on the plan selected.

  • Price changes: You will be notified through the App Store in advance of any price change.

All billing is managed by Apple. We do not process payments or store payment information.
