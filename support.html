Frequently asked questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription? ▼

Go to Profile → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

My AI analysis failed — what do I do? ▼

Check your internet connection and try again. Make sure the photo shows your full body in good lighting. If the issue persists, please email me with a description of the error.

Can I delete my data? ▼

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete everything, go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → Delete Account. You can also delete the app to remove all local data.

Does Gainly share my photos or health data? ▼

No. Photos are sent to the AI model only during analysis and are never stored on a server. Health data from Apple Health never leaves your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
